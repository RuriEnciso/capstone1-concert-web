Welcome to ON FIRE, the ultimate celebration of indie rock and roll! Get ready to ignite your passion for music as we bring you an electrifying festival experience like no other. Festival Highlights

Stellar Lineup

Enjoy an impressive lineup of renowned indie bands and emerging talents. From iconic artists to fresh discoveries, we guarantee you a unique musical experience.

Enchanting Stages

Experience the music in stunning outdoor settings that will transport you to a magical atmosphere. Our stages, surrounded by nature, create the perfect backdrop for the best indie rock sounds.

Interactive Activities

Immerse yourself in the indie culture by participating in music workshops, live painting sessions, and other interactive activities. Express yourself, connect with fellow music lovers, and let your creativity flow.

Artisan Market

Explore our curated market showcasing unique and creative products. Discover vintage clothing, handmade accessories, and one-of-a-kind treasures that complement your indie style.

Culinary Delights

Indulge in a variety of culinary options that will satisfy all your cravings. From food trucks to gourmet stalls, you'll find a wide range of flavors, including classic dishes and vegan/vegetarian choices.
